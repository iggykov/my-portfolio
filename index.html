About me

MY CHARACTER TYPE (ENFJ-A)

Having worked for years as a designer for the event and entertainment industry I developed many entrepreneurial qualities and a broad range of soft skills. I can easily fit into diverse teams and adapt rapidly to new circumstances. I love to spark enthusiasm in people around me. I am a creative but well-structured person and an honest, but empathetic communicator. This emphaty also helps me to understand and attend to users' as well as clients' needs. I like to take responsibility for difficult tasks and am able to stay calm and focused under pressure.


MY DESIGN PROCESS

Throughout the years, I developed a reliable design process that helps me to deliver quality results with respect to deadlines. Having conducted longterm projects, I learned how to balance overall progress and attention to details. I like working on projects that demand innovative thinking and flexibility.


THE UX/UI BOOTCAMP

I recently graduated UX/UI Design Bootcamp (April 2020) to adapt my design skills to the demands of the digital industry. I strongly believe that an inclusive, user-centered, and sustainable approach is profitable to any business and I want to contribute to that in my professional endevours.


MY DREAM JOB

I am looking for positions within dynamic teams of experts whom I can learn from and share knowledge with. I would like to take responsibility for setting the tone and visual language of digital products and immersive experiences. Because I have always been a curious adopter of new technologies and trends, I am especially interested in working within the SaaS industry and the AR (MR) environment on projects focused on creating innovative interactions and tools for brands and institutions.
